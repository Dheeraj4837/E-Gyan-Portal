Our Features

Empowering Education with Smart Solutions.

The NOU e-Gyan Portal is an innovative platform designed to streamline academic and administrative tasks. It includes a Student Information System for managing records, a secure Login Management System, an Enquiry Management System for handling queries, and a Complaint Management System for transparent grievance resolution. The Feedback Management System drives improvement through analyzed input, while the Study Management System organizes materials, schedules, and assignments.
